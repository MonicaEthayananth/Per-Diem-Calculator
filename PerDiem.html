Use this to calculate your meals and expense allowance (M&IE) in the following scenarios:

  1. The first day of travel is less than 10 hours.
  2. The last day of travel is less than 10 hours.
  3. The conference you are attending is providing one or more meals to you.

Instructions:

  • Fill out the Daily Per Diem Rate, Day of travel, and the number of meals provided.
  • Click Get Rates button to calculate results.
  • Click the Reset button to reset the calculator to default values.
  • For a full breakdown of calculations, click here.
